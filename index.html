Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work with Jira Cloud and Server?
Yes! Our extension supports both Jira Cloud (*.atlassian.net) and self-hosted Jira Server/Data Center instances.
Will this slow down my Jira board?
Not at all. The extension is lightweight and only activates when you toggle estimate mode. It has zero impact on normal Jira performance.
How do you handle custom story point fields?
We automatically detect your Jira instance's story point field, whether it's a standard field or a custom field with a different name.
Is my Jira data secure?
Absolutely. We don't store or transmit any of your Jira data to external servers. All operations happen locally in your browser using Jira's official API.
Can I customize the estimate values?
Yes! Pro users can configure custom estimate values (e.g., Fibonacci sequence, t-shirt sizes) to match their team's workflow.
What if I need help or have feature requests?
We offer email support for all users and priority support for Pro users. Feature requests can be submitted via our GitHub repository.
